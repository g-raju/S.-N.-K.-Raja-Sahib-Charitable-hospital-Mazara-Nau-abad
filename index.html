  • About Us

    History & Background

    • Shri Nabh Kanwal Raja Sahib Charitable Trust is one of the most well-renowned charitable institutions in the Doaba Region of Punjab. Located at village Mazara Nau Abad at 5km distance from Banga - Aur road, the Trust caters to the needs of the people from about 155 villages in the Banga Sub-tehsil of Nawanshahr district.

    • The trust is completely charitable organization run solely on the financial donations and cooperation received from the non-residents Indian (NRI) diasporasettled in various foreign countries. At present, the Trust operates a 75-bed hospital equipped with modern facilities and caters to the needy population of the region almost free-of-cost.


    Modest Beginning

    On 07-12-1990 honorable Sh. Amrik Singh Pooni(IAS) Finance Commissioner (Development) Punjab laid the foundation stone of the Trust. In 1990-91, the Hospital was started as a small dispensary in the building of Shri Nabh Kanwal Raja Sahib.


    Current Activities

    Today a hospital with 75-beds sits in a grand double-story building equipped with modern facilities and well-qualified staff at the service of the loacal people, offering treatment for a variety of simple and complex medical problems completely free of cost. The hospital was built and is operated solely from the donations of the NRI sangat.

    In addition, the trust supports a Briddh Ashram (old age home) hosting over 200 people, as well as the rasokhana- a community kitchen offering free meals to over 5000poor and destitute everyday.

    Mission

    The mission of Shri Nabh Kanwal Raja Sahib Memorial Trust is:

    • To serve humanity by offering highest quality medical services at nominal costs or free-of-cost to the population of the Doaba region.
    • To offer quality education from primary to senior secondary level, to the children of the needy from the Doaba region
    • To offer highest quality infrastructure and support system for de-addiction of local youth and to provide the avenues for development of vocational and professional skills.
    • To effectively manage all funds received in most professional, honest and transparent manner.

    Vission

    Shri Nabh Kanwal Raja Sahib Memorial Trust envisions:

    A prosperous Doaba region in which youth are healthy, well educated and skillful; where the old are respected and taken care of; where the poor and the needy have affordable and easy access to quality medical care as well as education for their children.

  • Services

    24x7 Services

    The complex is setup over an area of 4 acres constituting of main double storey hospital building, as well as residential quarters for doctors and paramedical staff. Such facilities for medical staff make possible a 24x7 service for the patients and the needy.

    Regular Medical Camps

    The Hospital conducts a variety of medical camps in its campus around the year.

    Since its inception, the hospital has been organizing two free eye checkup and operation camps every year in February/ March and October/ November. In every camp, 500- 1000 patients are inspected and operated upon with lense implanted. Further , these operations have been conducted with injections, stitches or bandages offered free of cost. Meals are also provided free of cost from the Rasokhana (Community Kitchen).

    Free medical check-up camp are also conducted in the hospital in which the lab test, X-Ray, and required medicines are provided free of cost serving about 1000 patients in each camp.

  • Facilities

    Free Medicines

    The slip fee for patients visiting in OPD has been fixed at a nominal Rs. 5 per visit. While regular medicines as well as medicines for common but expensive ailments are issued to the patients free of cost, while other specialized medicines are charged on a no-profit/ no-loss basis. For e.g Tuberculosis patients are given medicines free of cost. Moreover, poor and handicap patients receive completely free treatment.

    Modern Medical Facilities

    The Hospital is equipped with a number of modern medical equipment and facilities. These include:

    • General Surgery: For Gall Stone (Laparoscopic), T.U.R (Prostate Laparoscopic), Hernia and all types of appendicitis, Gastrointestinal treatment and operations
    • Eye, ear, nose, throat (E.N.T) related ailments and operations
    • Gynecological Operations, including normal and cesarean deliveries
    • Ortho disease surgery
    • Children disease examinations
    • Dental disease examinations
    • E.C.G, X-Ray, Spinal investigations(I.V.P, Banum shidy H.S.G etc)
    • Fully equipped laboratory and auto analyzers for laboratory Investigations
    • Ultra Sound examinations
    • 24 Hour Emergency room with equipment defrilator, Cardiac monitor, and Pulse Oxymeter
    • Diagnosis for heart disease, diabetes, tuberculosis, paralysis, blood pressure, and skin diseases
    • Operation theatre with latest equipment, including special arrangements for cardiac disease with E.C.G, Cardiac monitor, defrilator etc.
    • Ambulance with 24 hour service
    • 24 hour water and electricity
    • Facilities for General ward, Surgical ward, medical ward, Burn units, separate male and female ward, and private room
    75- Bed Capacity

    Currently, the Hospital has a capacity to accommodate 75 patients.

    Mortuary

    The Hospital also maintains a mortuary for 8 bodies.
